☕ HUAN. Café

💎 A Hidden Gem in Pampanga

📍 Location

🏫 The café “HUAN.” is located inside a famous local commercial estate called “Bart Mall” that is just at the front of the school “Holy Angel University”. People may have not discovered this café yet since it is found in a discreet location, only when you’re passing by the commercial estate, that’s when you will discover it. It’s like stumbling upon a hidden treasure chest along a dense forest. Everyone can get to go inside the café. Most of the people inside the café are students since it is nearby the University, and “Bart Mall” is often passed through by students.

💰 Price

💸 The foods and drinks are very affordable. While other cafés maybe a little too pricey, this café offers a great balance of quality and affordability. Having the foods and drinks in a reasonable price, making the café famous for students on a budget. The quality combined with the affordability of the menu, will make it a standout choice for everyone among the area.

🌟 Mood & Scenery

🛋️ The mood of the café is one of its key features. Having an aesthetically pleasing interior, with a modern yet cozy vibe, trendy and comfortable decor that will invite you to linger. It’s perfect for people who just wants to have a nice relaxing time whether you are working, studying or looking to catch up with your friends. The café offers a comfortable atmosphere, making it a pleasant environment to spend time in.

🍽️ Menu Highlights

🍵 Drinks

The café brags a fine range of drinks in the menu. It includes various coffees, lattes, mocha lattes, and they also serve non-coffee drinks. Throughout our visit, we ordered their Matcha Latte, Strawberry Matcha and their newly added drink, Seasalt Matcha. Their drinks were rich, perfectly sweetened, they also have a distinct striking good balance. Their Matcha Lattes, whether it’s their strawberry, or sea salt variations, they feature a smooth, creamy base with the earthy, slightly bitter notes of matcha; their Strawberry Matcha adds a refreshing sweetness and tang from strawberries, while their Seasalt Matcha introduces a savory twist with a hint of salt, enhancing the matcha's umami profile.

🍪 Food

In terms of food, they serve cookies and brownies. Their cookies offers diverse tastes. Their Chocolate Chip Cookie is known for its sweet, buttery flavor with hints of vanilla and rich chocolate chips that add a slightly bitter and melty contrast, creating a comforting experience. In contrast, a Red Velvet Cream Cheese Cookie combines a soft, mildly cocoa-flavored base with a tangy, creamy filling, resulting in a decadent and satisfying treat.

⏰ Time

⚡The service speed at the café is very commendable. The drinks arrived within just 5 minutes, which was impressively quick and allowed their customers to start enjoying their order almost immediately. In contrast, you can just buy the cookies since they are already made. The efficient service adds to the overall positive experience, ensuring that customers aren’t left waiting too long.

👥 Service & Atmosphere

⚡ Customer service is another area where the café excels. The staff were really friendly and attentive, making their customers feel welcome from the moment we walked in. They were very approachable and willing to answer any questions about the menu, and their positive attitude contributed to a warm and inviting atmosphere. It's clear that customer satisfaction is a priority here, and it shows in the way the staff interacts with their patrons.

🏆 Final Verdict

🌟 Overall, our experience at the café “HUAN.” was highly enjoyable. The combination of delicious food, great drinks, and a pleasant atmosphere made for a memorable visit. It's definitely a place that people would return to, whether for a study session, a casual hangout with friends, or just to enjoy another round of their tasty offerings. The blend of affordability, accessibility, and quality makes it a standout choice, and it's easy to see why it's a favourite among the students.
